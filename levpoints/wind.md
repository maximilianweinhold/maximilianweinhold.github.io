In the spirit of open source ecology, a wind turbine. 

Surprisingly only listed as @25% on the Global Village Construction Set. 

Constructable by a 2 hour a week team (1/N) over <3N years with 1000 quid and an overbudget, to give 1kW, just about continuously. 

A lot of parts are 3D printable (orderable), a lot of parts are $5 sensors. 

Mostly modular, with some hardware switch-outs for climate.
